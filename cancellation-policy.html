Overview

This Cancellation Policy explains how and when you can cancel a subscription or purchase made on the studybuddypremiumpro Academic Portal, owned and operated by Govind Lahu Ade (trading as StudyBuddyZone). All payments are processed through the PhonePe payment gateway.

Please read this policy carefully before completing any transaction on our platform. By proceeding with a payment, you acknowledge and agree to these cancellation terms.

Cancellation Before Payment

You may cancel your purchase at any time before completing payment — simply close or navigate away from the payment screen. No charges will be applied, and no further action is required.

If a payment is initiated via PhonePe but not completed (pending/timeout), the transaction will auto-cancel within 30 minutes and any deducted amount will be automatically refunded by PhonePe.

Cancellation After Payment

Once a payment has been successfully processed, the following cancellation terms apply:

  • Within 12 hours of payment: Cancellation with a full refund is allowed. Please contact us immediately at studybuddyzone.help.in@gmail.com or +91 9049321230
  • After 12 hours of payment: Cancellation is not eligible for a refund once portal access has been granted and the 12-hour window has passed
  • Technical / duplicate payment: Cancellations due to technical errors or double charges are handled separately — refer to our Refund Policy

The 12-hour window starts from the timestamp of your successful payment confirmation, not from when you first access the content.

Non-Cancellable Situations

  • Cancellation requests made more than 12 hours after a successful payment
  • Accounts where content has been extensively accessed or downloaded
  • Requests initiated to exploit the cancellation policy in bad faith
  • Cases where the same user has previously requested a cancellation/refund for the same plan

PhonePe Gateway & Cancellations

All payment cancellations and refunds on studybuddypremiumpro are routed through the PhonePe Payment Gateway. We do not have access to your UPI PIN, card details, or net banking credentials at any point.

For payment disputes, you may also contact PhonePe support directly via their app under the "Help" section if our response is delayed beyond 48 hours.
